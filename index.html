About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screen for a more comfortable viewing height. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture and make more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the task at hand.

Featuring artisan craftmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
